How we work

Four principles that don't bend.

I

Skin in the game

We advise from the same seat we invest from. When we back a company or a board, our own capital and reputation are on the line — so our counsel is never academic.

II

Negotiation is the core craft

Every valuation, every board vote, every partnership is a negotiation. We treat it as a discipline — built on the Harvard framework and thirty years of practice.

III

Board-grade rigour

Decisions are structured, evidence is mapped, stakeholders are read. We bring the same intelligence to a portfolio company that a chair brings to a boardroom.

IV

Discretion by default

The work is confidential, the relationships are long, and the names stay private. Trust is the asset that compounds fastest.

The Negodom tool — MyBoards

Decision intelligence for people who sit on many boards.

Serving three to eight boards means holding more context than any one mind can track. Board-Intelligence ingests board packs, news and your own notes, then surfaces what matters: cross-board patterns, commitment tracking, coalition maps and preparation briefs.

It is how our advisory scales — and a capability we extend to a select group of directors and executives.
